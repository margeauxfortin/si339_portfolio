Welcome to Fresh & Friendly Foods! I hope you try one of our recipes and that we can help add a little something new to your vegan or vegan curious food journey :)

Who am I?

Hi guys, name is Margeaux Fortin. I am the chef, photographer, and creater behind this blog and I am so excited to share my journey with you. I have been vegan for 7 years and throughout the process have loved learning about the diet and developing recipes to challenge my cooking skills and tastes. Vegan food is incredible, diverse, flavorful, and full of spices and ingredients I had never tried previously, and that's why I use it as a platform to open people'e minds to the lifestyle. Try some of my recipes and let me know what you think!
