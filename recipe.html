Simple Shortbread Cookies with Fruit Filling

These cookies are the perfect summer dessert for any occasion or just to have around the house! They are so yummy and incredibly easy to make :)

RECIPE:

SHORTBREAD COOKIE:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup (8 Tbs) vegan butter, softened
  • 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 Tbs nut-milk, as needed
  • 1 tsp salt

LEMON CURD

  • 1 cup unsweetened non-dairy milk (I like oat)
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 Tbs lemon zest
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 Tbs cornstarch
  • 2 Tbs vegan butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric for color
  • pinch of salt

GLAZE:

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 Tbs non-dairy milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon extract
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. First, prepare the lemon curd, or any other fruit filling of your choice (you can also use store-bought jams if you want it to be even easier!).
  2. For the lemon curd just add all ingredients to a pot over medium heat, whisking frequently until the mixture thickens (5-10 minutes). Then set aside to cool overnight, or if needed soon place in the fridge.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 and lightly grease a baking sheet with vegan butter.
  4. Then for the cookies, mix the flour and powdered sugar together in a large bowl. Add the vegan butter and vanilla and mix with an electric mixer until combined well. The texture should be crumbly but combine when squeezed in your hand, if it‚Äôs too dry add a splash of non-dairy milk and mix again.
  5. Shape the dough into small balls, about 1 Tbs each, and place on cookie sheet. Then gently indent the center of each cookie, making a place for the lemon curd.
  6. Fill the cookies with your cooled lemon curd (or other fruit fillings), then bake for 10-12 minutes. Don‚Äôt worry if they appear underdone, they will firm up as they cool!
  7. Make the glaze by whisking the powdered sugar and almond milk together in a small bowl. Then divide the mixture in half and add the vanilla to one half and the lemon extract to the other.
  8. Once the cookies have cooled completely, drizzle the glaze over the tops of your cookies as you chose. Let the glaze set for a few minutes and enjoy!
